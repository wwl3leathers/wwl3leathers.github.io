Your idea can be anything—if it’s not in the Shop, it lives here. Dream it up, I’ll wrangle the leather.

Custom Project Deposits:
Once we finalize your design together, I’ll send you a secure Square invoice for a 30% non-refundable deposit. Square also gives you the option to save your card on file. When your item is finished, I’ll send the final invoice (or charge the saved card after confirming with you) before shipping.

Non-Refundable Deposits & Payments: Leather can’t be “unstained,” “unstamped,” or “un-stitched.” Once we begin, all payments and deposits are non-refundable. (And no, you can’t return a “vintage” stain either.)
